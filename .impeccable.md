# Design Context

## Users
- **Primary Audience**: Students and Tech Lovers.
- **Context**: Learning about LLMs and using an agent for daily tasks or educational purposes.
- **Job to be Done**: Interact with an LLM agent efficiently, manage memory, and experiment with RAG.
- **Emotions**: Simplicity, convenience, and a friendly but professional "technical" vibe.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice & Tone**: Friendly, simple, and convenient.
- **3-Word Personality**: Simple, Convenient, Friendly.
- **Emotional Goals**: Empowered, focused, and curious.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Technical & Moody.
- **Theme**: Dark mode preferred with high-contrast accents (vibrant blue or technical colors).
- **Inspiration**: Developer tools, high-end IDEs, and modern "tech-focused" interfaces that aren't overly clinical but feel "pro."
- **Anti-references**: Overly generic "SaaS-blue" corporate designs, cluttered/noisy layouts, or "friendly" rounded-everything designs that lose their technical edge.

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity through Contrast**: Use deep dark backgrounds with high-contrast, technical accents to guide focus.
2. **Intentional Hierarchy**: Make important technical information stand out with bold typography or subtle glow effects.
3. **Frictionless Convenience**: Keep the layout simple and intuitive, ensuring key actions are always within reach.
4. **Moody but Friendly**: Use a dark palette that feels "technical" but softened by subtle gradients or friendly micro-interactions.
